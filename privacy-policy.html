Section 03

TikTok API Data

Our App integrates with TikTok's official API. When you authorize our App to connect with your TikTok account, we may access:

  • Your TikTok public profile information (username, display name, avatar)
  • Your TikTok videos and related metadata (as authorized)
  • Analytics and performance data for your content (as authorized)
  • Comments and engagement data (as authorized)
  • View basic profile info, video list, and campaign analytics

We access only the scopes you explicitly approve during the TikTok OAuth authorization flow. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings at TikTok Connected Apps.

TikTok API data is used solely to provide the features of this App. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.
